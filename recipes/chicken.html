Chicken Recipe

Decription

This baked chicken recipe is a simple yet flavorful way to enjoy tender and juicy chicken. Seasoned with a mix of aromatic spices and baked to perfection, this dish is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 lbs)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 4 cloves of garlic, crushed

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Clean the chicken by removing any excess fat and giblets, then pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Rub the chicken with olive oil, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Mix the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika in a small bowl and generously season the chicken inside and out.
  5. Place the lemon halves, rosemary sprigs, and crushed garlic cloves inside the chicken cavity for added flavor.
  6. Truss the chicken by tying the legs together with kitchen twine. This helps it cook evenly.
  7. Place the chicken breast-side up in a roasting pan or oven-safe dish.
  8. Roast in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 20 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C) when checked at the thickest part of the meat.
  9. Baste the chicken every 20 minutes with its juices for extra crispiness and flavor.
  10. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving.
  11. Serve and enjoy your perfectly roasted chicken!
